Shazzy The Bachelor

The project aims to convey the message that hard work ultimately pays off. Using the story of an individual called Shazzy who is graduating high school, the user gets to make decisions on future decisions that Shazzy should make. There are four different paths that the user can choose and therefore four different versions of Shazzy’s story. The use of different storylines in the comic allows the user to investigate the different storylines and ultimately understand the moral of the story, hard work ultimately pays off and is the reason behind having a successful career.

No Room for Suri

‘No Room for Suri’ is a situational comedy centred around the life of Suri, an easygoing guy who often finds himself in the most unfortunate of situations. In the end, he is stuck between a rock and a hard place to determine his next roommate.
